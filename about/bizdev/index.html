Business Development

The Business Development teams work to help maintain our organization, support our 5 STEMchats programs, and connect our work with the public.

External Development

The External Development Team collaborates with all the STEMchats teams to handle any external initiatives to progress public-facing aspects of STEMchats in addition to solidifying our place in the STEM, education, nonprofit, and social impact spaces! This includes helping plan events (virtual or in-person), conducting partnership outreach and maintaining the partnership database, and any other various external initiatives.

Currently, External Development is conducting promotional outreach for our virtual events, helping to manage STEMchats University Chapters, outlining methods to make our platforms and programming more accessible, developing a Sponsorship Prospectus to categorize partnerships, and working with STEMchats’ Speaker Series Initiative to build a network of audience members and prospective speakers.

This work is critical to the success of STEMchats since a majority of our impact occurs through public-facing programming and initiatives.

Internal Development

The Internal Development Team works on making sure everything runs smoothly within STEMchats. As a nonprofit organization that aims to have a large impact on the STEM field, we work to make sure all of our teams work efficiently while maintaining a strong team culture.

This is achieved through identifying Key Performance Metrics (KPIs) and then working around these to submit internal audit reports, which include recommendations for the numerous teams.

Other projects include team socials, assisting the Leadership Team with onboarding and recruitment, member evaluations, and undertaking various new initiatives, such as the Internal Newsletter and creating a professional network for all members of the organization.

Funding

The Funding Team focuses on the financial aspects of STEMchats to ensure that all programs and initiatives run successfully without having to endure any financial burdens - much like our mission to provide equitable education at no cost!

The Funding team stays in constant communication with all five programs to prepare accordingly by creating budget lists, researching applicable grants, reaching out to companies/organizations for sponsorships, brainstorming alternative fundraising methods, and assisting with donations and donor relations.

While the main objective is to obtain money, Funding works with both the Marketing and External Development teams to build a lasting relationship with our sponsors/partners, reach and captivate new and existing audiences, and ultimately, establish a financially sustainable nonprofit to accomplish our mission!

Marketing

The Marketing Team strives to help our audiences through education and promotion. This means educating STEMchats social media audiences through a range of marketing campaigns all while promoting STEMchats programs and opportunities.

While the primary focus is on creating graphics, videos, and general posts on social media, Marketing also helps with any other graphic design needs in order to maintain an online presence that accurately depicts the STEMchats’ brand and our values + mission.

So far, the Marketing team has (1) established a branding guide for our overall brand as well as for 4 out of 5 of our programs (2) conducted 5 general STEMchats campaigns and (3) conducted 5 program-related campaigns.

Technology

The Technology Team utilizes their collective technical skills to support each aspect of the STEMchats organization. Through the creation of web applications, online resources, and forms of digital communication, Technology seeks to raise awareness of STEM opportunities for FGLI communities. Since launching the STEMchats official website, the Technology team has created platforms for the STEMlights newsletter, the Impact Ideathon event, as well as the i-STEM Scholar's educational resources.

Through Technology's main focus to create and amplify the platforms for STEMchats’ programs so that students anywhere can learn more about them, the Tech team has built strong interpersonal and technical skills, seeking always to learn new things and to form a conducive environment. Being in constant communication with other programs, Technology strives to highlight the multifaceted programs that STEMchats offers. Parallel to the Tech team's accomplishments, Technology is constantly challenging themselves with future endeavours to improve STEMchats with the best technology.
